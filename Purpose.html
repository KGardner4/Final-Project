OUR PURPOSE





The problem to solve is the loss of diversity in fashion. Within our site we give the user a brief summary regarding how the three of us would describe our individual style. In addition, we include a style quiz as a fun interactive tool for one to receive a personalized description of their style. Lastly, our search bar leads the user to a website where it lists the top 20 colleges where Fashion Design is available as a major. According to Data USA, the most common ethnicity in Fashion Design are Caucasian people, second are Latinos, and lastly African-Americans. Our website's purpose is give users the initial step to find out their style---hopefully giving them more personalised style advice and secondly it's meant to encourage those who are older, such as: high school seniors and interested in Fashion Design to pursue their pasttime as a future career choice; in turn, increasing the level of diversity in the fashion world!!!
